THE MUNICIPALITY OF SOFRONIO ESPAÑOLA

Vision

"Sofronio Española is envisioned as agro-industrial and ecotourism destination with God-fearing, empowered and culturally enriched community living in an ecologically balanced environment and progressive economy supported by resilient infrastructure and strong dynamic, transparent and just governance.”

Mission

“Sofronio Española is committed to promote self-reliance, integrating multi-sectoral approaches, participatory governance, enhancing ecological balance to attain economic, socio-cultural and political stability for better quality of life.”

OFFICE OF THE SANGGUNIANG BAYAN

The Office of the Sangguniang Bayan, as the legislative arm of the municipal government, provides support to the Executive Department through the enactment of measures (ordinances and resolutions) crucial to the realization of the predefined goals and objectives of the LGU which are geared towards improving the delivery of its basic services thereby effecting positive change on the quality of life of the people.

OFFICE OF THE MUNICIPAL PLANNING AND DEVELOPMENT COORDINATOR

The Municipal Planning and Development Office (MPDO) oversees and coordinates the municipality’s strategic planning and development activities. It is responsible for laying out the plan for advancement and actively engages in comprehensive planning to maximize resources and guide the community toward sustainable growth. By organizing numerous activities, the office acts as significant catalyst in the development of a dynamic and effective municipality.

MUNICIPAL BUDGET OFFICE

The Municipal Budget Officer provides technical services in budgeting to the Local Chief Executive, other Municipal Officials, and Barangay Officials as well. It exercises general supervision over the municipal and barangay budgets and responsible in formulating the local budget to reflect local government goals and objectives.

OFFICE OF THE MUNICPAL TREASURER

The Municipal Treasurer is tasked to collect all revenues, take custody and exercise proper management and disbursement of funds in accordance with existing laws, rules, and regulations, maintain and update tax information system, inspect business enterprises in relation to implement of tax ordinances and advice the Sangguniang Bayan and the Local Chief Executive about the local government funds and other contingent activities and services needed by the municipality. It also plays a vital role in managing the financial affairs of our municipal or local government. Overall, the municipal treasurer’s office plays a critical role in safeguarding the financial health and stability of the municipality, ensuring the resources are managed efficiently and effectively to support the delivery of essential services to residents.

OFFICE OF THE MUNICIPAL ACCOUNTANT

The Municipal Accounting Office takes charge of both the accounting and internal audit services of the Municipal Government including nine (9) barangays within its jurisdiction. Aside from submitting interim and year-end financial statement to the Local Chief Executive and Sangguniang Bayan, it also tasked to appraise the Local Government Officials on the financial conditions and operations of the Local Government Unit, maintain and keep all records and reports related thereto and review supporting documents of disbursement vouchers and liquidation reports to determine completeness of requirements. We are also in-charge of various Trust Fund account given by the National Government Agencies such as Projects under the Local Government Support Fund-Support to Barangay Development Program (LGSF-SBDP) from Department of Interior and Local Government (DILG); Health Facility Enhancement Program and various projects from Department of Health (DOH); various projects from Department of Agriculture (DA); Social Pension and Supplementary Feeding Program from Department of Social Welfare and Development (DSWD); and projects from other national government agencies. We are also handling the account of South Palawan Planning Council Mt. Mantalingahan Protected Landscape Project of five (5) different municipalities namely Brooke’s Point, Rizal, Quezon, Aborlan, and Sofronio Española is under our office as Mayor Abner Rafael N. Tesorio is being the chairman of Impok.

HUMAN RESOURCE MANAGEMENT OFFICE

The Human Resource Management Office takes charge in recruitment and examination, selection, promotion, performance evaluation, appointment preparation, leave administration, personnel relation, discipline, benefits, grants and incentives, retirement and career development of local government employees. It also facilitated the conduct of screening and assessment of applicants who applied for certain position and published during the period through the HRM Personnel Selection Board (PSB) and processed appointments to the Civil Service Commission Field Office.

OFFICE OF THE MUNICIPAL ASSESSOR

The Office of the Municipal Assessor tasked with the continuous assessment of real properties within the jurisdiction of the locality concerned, established a systematic method of assessment, prepare install and maintain a real property identification system of tax mapping showing graphically all properties subject to assessment in the municipality and gather all necessary data concerning the same. Make frequent physical surveys to check and determine whether all real properties within the locality are properly listed in the assessment rolls. Update and maintain assessment records. Prepare monthly and quarterly reports, plans and programs, action plan to be submitted to Provincial Assessor’s Office and assisted property owner in filing sworn statement for the current and fair market values of their properties.

OFFICE OF THE MUNICIPAL ENGINEER

The Engineering Office prepare4d plans, programs of works and other technical documents, and administered the implementation of all infrastructure projects of the municipality comprising of nine (9) barangays. These projects include delegated projects from the national government thru Aid to Municipalities-Local Government Support Fund and Support to barangay Development Program (AM-LGSF-SBDP), a program of the Department of Interior and Local Government (DILG) and other programs from different agencies of the government like the Kilusan Laban sa Kahirapan Program (KALAHI) of the Department of Social Welfare and Development (DSWD) and Department of Health (DOH) where preparation of detailed engineering design and technical supervision is also a routine work for us. Aside from routine responsibilities, the municipal engineer also acts as building official who is responsible for the approval and issuance of building and occupancy permits for all buildings to be constructed within the municipality. It also tasked to manage the day-to-day operation and maintenance of heavy equipment under the motor pool division of this office. Minor construction and regular maintenance of municipal and barangay roads were usually done by administration. Maintenance and major repair of all government vehicles were also done in the motor pool.

BUSINESS PERMIT AND LICENSING SECTION

The Business Permit and Licensing Section provides effective and efficient system, procedures and practices in the issuance and renewal of business and occupational permits. It is also responsible for regulating the nature and or operations of various business activities within the municipality.

MUNICIPAL ECONOMIC ENTERPRISE AND DEVELOPMENT OFFICE

The Municipal Economic Enterprise and Development Office oversees and ensure the management and administration effectively and efficiently. Its composition are Public Market, Slaughterhouse, Terminal Management Unit and Water Management Unit. The office is responsible for market operation and collection, stall rental collection, slaughterhouse collection, cash ticket collection and collection of water bills.

MUNCIPAL ENVIRONMENT AND NATURAL RESOURCES

The Municipal Environment and Natural Resources Office is created thru Ordinance No. 2019-158, its mandate is to implement Local Government environmental programs/projects and enforce local ordinances and other national environmental laws. It is also responsible for the delivery of basic services and other related activities of the local government unit. Managing natural resources, maintaining ecological balance, protecting, and preservation of the remaining forest is the office’s primary objectives and the provision of technical support to the Executive and Legislative branch of the Local Government, recommending key development investment program, and coordinate to the National Government and other Non-government organization (NGO). MENRO is tasked to protect and conserve the Municipality’s environment and natural resources and implement program and projects of the municipal environment related to the sustainable development of the environment and natural resources.

MUNICIPAL COOPERATIVE DEVELOPMENT OFFICE

MUNCIPAL GENERAL SERVICES OFFICE

Municipal General Services Office shall help develops plans and strategies and provides technical assistance to the mayor in delivery of basic services which require general services expertise and technical support.
